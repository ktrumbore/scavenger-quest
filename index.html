Welcome

Saturday, September 16

Ahoy, adventurers of the high seas! Welcome to Scavenger Quest, the ultimate outdoor scavenger hunt in the heart of Old City, Philadelphia. Join us on an exhilarating journey where the city streets are treasure-laden. Get ready to decipher cryptic clues, solve mind-bending puzzles, and race against other daring crews, all while exploring the rich history and hidden gems of this historic city. Are you ready to embark? Join us and let the adventure begin!

This adventure is rain or shine.

Your Quest

Unearth Treasures Together!

Gather your crew and prepare to set forth! At Scavenger Quest, teamwork is the name of the game, and you'll form teams of up to six members to conquer the challenge together.

As you explore the historic streets, you'll encounter real actors who are seamlessly woven into the fabric of the event. These characters will provide valuable clues, interact with you in character, and guide your team on your quest for glory. And remember, as the clock ticks, keep an eye on the leaderboard to see how your team fares against the competition.

Event Agenda

CHECK-IN

Begins at 11:00am

Teams will meet and check-in at our quest starting point, Rotten Ralph's Pub & Eatery, in Old City Philadelphia.

Pick up your novelty t-shirt, access our interactive online web app, and get ready for unlimited fun!

HUNT & RACE

12:00pm to 3:00pm

Find clues and crack codes that lead you to your next destination while you race the other teams to the finish line!

Be the first to finish and win a prize! Awards will also be given for best pirate-themed costumes and best group picture.

AFTER-PARTY

Begins at 3:30pm

Join us at Rotten Ralph's Pub & Eatery after the quest! Enjoy 15% off your total bill the day of the event.
