FAQ

Have questions? We have a treasure trove of answers.

Is this different from the Philadelphia Escapely Box?

The clues, puzzles, and solves are all new! Those familiar with Escapely will find this event to be the same quality, but with all new content and some added surprises. Plus, there's a winner!

What is included in my ticket when I register?

Each individual tickets includes: 1 ticket to play in the Philadelphia Scavenger Quest, 1 novelty t-shirt, 1 access code to our interactive web app (nothing to download!), and unlimited fun!

How far will I walk during the event?

The entire event takes place on foot. You will walk no more than 2-3 miles over the course of the event, depending on which paths you take and how quickly you find the clues.

What ages can participate in this event?

This event is fun for all ages! Each team is required to have at least one player over the age of 18, however, and the after-party will have alcoholic beverages for those over 21.

Are tickets for Scavenger Quest transferable?

Yes. To transfer a ticket to another user, please email us at hello@escapely.com and include your confirmation number. From there, we can manage your transfer.

Will food or drinks be provided?

We encourage you to bring water, food, and snacks. There will be plenty of opportunities to purchase and enjoy food and drinks along the race course!

What do I need to bring?

Wear comfortable shoes and clothing, bring a fully charged mobile device, and you may find a pen and paper helpful for scratch work (though it is not required).

What happens if it rains?

This event will be hosted rain or shine. Please dress accordingly.

What is the refund policy?

Tickets may be transferred to other players, but there are no refunds.
